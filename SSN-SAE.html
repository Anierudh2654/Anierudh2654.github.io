About Us

The official student-led automotive engineering team of SSN College of Engineering. We are a passionate group of innovators, designers, and engineers committed to pushing the boundaries of automotive technology. Specializing in quad bikes, go-karts, and modified vehicles, we apply cutting-edge engineering principles to design and fabricate high-performance machines for national-level competitions.

Mission

Fueling passion, engineering innovation! We design, build, and race high-performance vehicles, pushing the limits of technology while shaping future automotive leaders.

Vision

To be a leading collegiate automotive team recognized for engineering excellence, innovation, and sustainability. We strive to inspire future engineers, contribute to the advancement of mobility solutions, and create a legacy of success in national and international SAE competitions.
